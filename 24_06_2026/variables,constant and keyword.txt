what is variables?
ans- A variable is a contanier that stores a 'values'
example:
a=10; // a is assigned 10

Rules for nameing variables in C
(1) The first character must be an alphabet or underscore(_).
(2) No commas,no blanks space allow.
(3) No special symbol other then underscore is allow.
(4) variable names are case sensitive.

what is constants?
ans- An entity whose value does not change is called a constant.

Type of constant
(1) Integer constant = -1,6,4,7.
(2) Real constant = -3.3331,2.5,7.0.
(3) character constant = 'a', '$', '@'.
